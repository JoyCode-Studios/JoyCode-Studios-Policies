Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 11, 2025

Typely respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email address, display name, and authentication provider (Google, GitHub, or Email).
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, login timestamps, and interaction logs (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Content: Posts, comments, and any data you submit to Typely.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate users and secure access to Typely features.
  • To monitor usage trends and detect abuse or violations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We use platforms like Firebase and Cloudflare to operate Typely securely and reliably. This means we do not manage data and some data like passwords are inaccessible to us.

4. Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and associated data at any time.
  • You can request access to your stored data by contacting us.
  • You can opt out of analytics tracking via browser settings.

5. Security

Typely uses secure authentication, encrypted tokens, and backend verification to protect your data.

6. Contact

If you have questions or concerns, email us at support@joycode.co.uk.

7. General

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.

We may review certain posts on Typely to ensure compliance with our guidelines and policies.

Typely is a modular publishing platform built with privacy, agency, and creative ownership in mind.
