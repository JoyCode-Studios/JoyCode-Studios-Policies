Typely Content Guidelines

Typely is a creative space for sharing ideas, stories, and thoughtful posts. To keep it safe and welcoming for everyone, especially younger users, we ask that all content follows these guidelines.

1. Age Suitability

All content must be suitable for users aged 10 and up. That means no violence, hate speech, explicit material, or anything that could be harmful or scary to younger readers.

2. Respectful Communication

  • Be kind and constructive in your posts and comments.
  • No bullying, harassment, or targeting individuals or groups.
  • Respect others’ privacy — don’t share personal information.

3. Moderation & Review

Some posts may be reviewed by moderators before they appear publicly. This helps us keep Typely safe and aligned with our community values.

4. Reporting Content

If you see a post that breaks these guidelines, you can report it using the Report button next to the post. Our team will review it and take appropriate action.

5. Creative Freedom

Typely celebrates creativity — you’re welcome to share poems, puzzles, stories, and thoughtful commentary. Just keep it respectful, safe, and age-appropriate.

6. Questions?

If you're unsure whether something is okay to post, feel free to ask us at support@joycode.co.uk.

Typely is built for thoughtful expression, safe sharing, and creative ownership. Let’s keep it kind.
